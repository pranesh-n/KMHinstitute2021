About Dr. Kamakshi Institute of Medical Science and Research

The Dr. Kamakshi Institute of Medical Sciences & Research (KIMSR) was established in the year 2010 by Dr.T.G.Govindarajan with a vision of generating required human resources for the healthcare industry. It is a unique academic institution that is committed to academic excellence. The institute is an additional facility of Dr. Kamakshi Memorial Hospital Pvt. Ltd., founded in 2005, with a vision “to serve and deliver every patient with the ultimate excellence and devotion, as we care for our loved ones”. The hospital is a 300 bedded Multi Super Specialty hospital at Pallikaranai, situated in the southern part of Chennai, spread over 60 ground land and 75000 square ft building area.

The KIMSR is affiliated with the Tamil Nadu Dr. M. G. R Medical University under the allied health sciences stream. The various courses conducted are BSc Radiography and Imaging Technology (RIT), BSc Radiotherapy technology (RTT), and BSc Medical Laboratory Technology (MLT). The courses are of 4-year duration, including a 3-year course and a one-year internship. The departments are headed by highly experienced teachers with rich clinical experience as well as in technology. The RIT and RTT courses are duly approved by Atomic Energy Regulatory Board, Govt. of India, so that our students can work throughout India. As of 2019, 185 students have graduated, including 118 from Radiography and Imaging Technology, 52 from Radiotherapy technology and 15 from Medical laboratory Technology. All the students have got good placement both in India and abroad and earning several thousand to lakhs INR as a perk.

The KIMSR is also conducting Diploma in National board courses (DNB,2011) on Radiodiagnosis and Family medicine and so far 11 candidates have successfully passed the DNB courses and all are well employed. The institute is also approved by Anna University, Chennai, as an R&D institute, to conduct research on Medical physics speciality. At present, 3 students are presuming their PhD program in the University. In addition, the institute offers implant training, short and long term training programs for Engineering, Nursing, Medical physics students of various other institutions and colleges in and around Chennai. In Medical Physics itself, about 48, 35, 8 students of various Universities have completed their implant training, project dissertation and one-year internship, respectively.

Recently, the institute has started M.Sc., Radiology and Imaging Technology course under Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R Medical University, and many more such courses are in the pipeline. Similarly, few more DNB courses will be started shortly on rare specialities.
