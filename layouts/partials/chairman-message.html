Chairman's Message

My dear students, parents, families, staff and alumni,

We cannot always build the future for our youth, but we can build our youth for the future

-- Franklin D Roosevelt

I believe in certain values of life; honesty, equality, Justice, dedication, courage, loyalty, Integration and Commitment. These qualities are inbuilt within all of us. You have to only join Dr. Kamakshi Institute of Medical Sciences and Research to bring out the above-said qualities and to expertise and identify the innate talents within you.

To follow the footsteps of my teaches and great medical professionals, we should follow the following tenets:

  1. The safety, esteem and welfare of your patients come first every time
  2. Safety, esteem and welfare of your Command comes next
  3. Safety, self-esteem and welfare of your own come last.

Our constant endeavour is to create an ambience in the campus propitious for the academic development of the students. The Institution is a “Smoke-Free Zone” and strict discipline is maintained within the campus. We firmly believe in excellence and discipline, which is not an act, but a habit working as perhaps catalysts and facilitators of development. Blessed are also the parents who have sent their wards to our prestigious institution, which would be the envy of many because this institution imports the kinds of knowledge and education which many of us strive for. Our strong desire is to provide a comprehensive educational system rather than just producing graduates. I can proudly say that our students will graduate with good knowledge and values. I extend my best wishes to all those seeking admission to this institution and assure all of them that their stay in this institution will be highly rewarding, both academically and spiritually.
