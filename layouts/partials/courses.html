Courses

Bachelor of Science Programs

Duration: 3 Years + 1-year internship

  • Radiography and Imaging Technology
  • Medical Laboratory Technology
  • Radiotherapy Technology
    Eligiblity
  • Candidate shall be completed the age of 17 years on or before 31st December of the year of admission to the course.
  • The Maximum age limit for admission is 30 years on or before 31st December of the year of admission to the course.
  • He/She has passed the H.S.C / (10+2) or equivalent Examination from a recognized board / University in India with Minimum aggregate of 50 % Marks in Physics, Chemistry and Biology/ Botany + Zoology provided the candidate has passed in each subject separately.

Masters of Science Programs

Duration: 2 Years

  • Radiography and Imaging Technology
    Eligiblity
  • The student should have completed the age 21 years on or before 31st December of the year of admission to the course.
  • Bachelor of Radiology / Radiography and Imaging Technology or its equivalent (3years + 1 year of Internship) from a UGC recognized University with Minimum 50% Marks or CGPA of 5.0
  • Candidates who are Physically fit and Mentally sound to undergo the academic course and does not suffer from any intellectual disability or contagious disease.
